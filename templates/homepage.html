What is SlateCMS?

SlateCMS is a powerful backend-based content management system (CMS) designed to streamline content organization, editing, and publishing. It allows developers and content teams to manage website or app content dynamically without interfering with the frontend structure. Manage product descriptions, pricing, and promotions dynamically. Schedule and update articles without frontend modifications. Keep company profiles, events, and announcements updated. Simplify your website management with ease and speed. Deliver backend-driven content updates without app releases. It provides a user-friendly interface for managing blog posts, pages, media, users, and SEO settings, all from a single dashboard. With its API-first approach, it seamlessly integrates with front-end frameworks like React, Vue, and Next.js.

Key Features

  • API-First Approach: Integrates seamlessly with front-end frameworks via REST or GraphQL APIs.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Manage permissions for admins, editors, and contributors.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Multiple users can edit content with version tracking.
  • Automated Publishing & Scheduling: Set publish dates and automate content workflows.
  • Media Library: Upload, store, and optimize images and videos for web use.
  • SEO Optimization Tools: Built-in metadata fields and keyword recommendations.
