What is Blicky?

Blicky is a powerful backend-based content management system (CMS) designed to streamline content organization, editing, and publishing. It allows developers and content teams to manage website or app content dynamically without interfering with the frontend structure. Manage product descriptions, pricing, and promotions dynamically. Schedule and update articles without frontend modifications. Keep company profiles, events, and announcements updated. Simplify your website management with ease and speed. Deliver backend-driven content updates without app releases.

Key Features

  • API-First Approach: Integrates seamlessly with front-end frameworks via REST or GraphQL APIs.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Manage permissions for admins, editors, and contributors.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Multiple users can edit content with version tracking.
  • Automated Publishing & Scheduling: Set publish dates and automate content workflows.
  • Media Library: Upload, store, and optimize images and videos for web use.
